About Conference

ComSIA 2024 is organised with the objective of bringing together innovative scientists, professors, research scholars, students and industrial experts in the field of Sciences, Computational Methods and Techniques to a common platform. The primary goal of the conference is to promote the exchange of innovative scientific information between researchers, developers, engineers, students, and practitioners. Overall the conference will provide the researchers and attendees with prospects for national and international collaboration and networking among universities and institutions from India and abroad for promoting research. ComSIA 2024 will be jointly organized by School of Open Learning, University of Delhi and Shaheed Rajguru College of Applied Sciences, University of Delhi on 10-11 May, 2024. All the accepted papers are published by Springer Scopus Indexed Series (Proposd) & CEUR Workshop Proceedings (SCOPUS & DBLP Indexed) and some selected papers will be published in the following SCOPUS/WoS/DBLP/ACM indexed Journals.

1. Intelligent Decision Technologies, IOS Press, SCOPUS, ESCI
2. Alexandria Engineering Journal, Elsevier, SCI
3. Internet Technology Letters, SCOPUS, ESCI
4. IEEE transactions on Consumer Electronics, SCI
